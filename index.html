Food Choices


Breakfast:

  • Option 1- Oatmeal with Berries:

    • Oatmeal topped with fresh mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries).
    A drizzle of honey or a sprinkle of cinnamon for added flavor.

  • Option 2 - Yogurt Parfait:

    • Greek yogurt with granola and a mix of sliced bananas and kiwi.
    A small handful of nuts (e.g., almonds or walnuts) for extra crunch.


    Lunch:

  • Option 1- Turkey and Veggie Wrap:

    • Whole wheat tortilla filled with lean turkey slices, lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumber.
    A side of baby carrots and hummus for dipping.

  • Option 2 - Veggie and Cheese Quesadilla:

    • A whole wheat quesadilla filled with saut√©ed bell peppers, onions, and low-fat cheese.
    A side of salsa for dipping.


    Snack:

  • Option 1- Apple Slices with Peanut Butter:

    • Sliced apples served with a small cup of natural peanut or almond butter.
    A few baby carrots for an extra crunchy snack.

  • Option 2 - Greek Yogurt with Honey:

    • A serving of Greek yogurt with a drizzle of honey.
    A sprinkle of mixed nuts (e.g., walnuts and almonds) for added texture.


    Dinner:

  • Option 1- Baked Chicken Tenders:

    • Baked chicken tenders coated in whole wheat breadcrumbs and herbs.
    A side of steamed broccoli and brown rice.

  • Option 2 - Veggie Stir-Fry:

    • A serving of Greek yogurt with a drizzle of honey.
    A colorful stir-fry with tofu or lean chicken, mixed vegetables (bell peppers, snap peas, carrots),
    and a light soy or teriyaki sauce.
    Served over brown rice.


    Dessert:

  • Option 1- Fruit Salad:

    • A refreshing fruit salad with a mix of seasonal fruits like watermelon, cantaloupe, and pineapple.
    A sprinkle of shredded coconut for extra flavor.

  • Option 2 - Frozen Banana Pops:

    • Sliced bananas on popsicle sticks, dipped in yogurt, and rolled in crushed granola.
    Freeze until firm for a tasty and healthy treat.
